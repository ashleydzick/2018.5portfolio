Adjunct Professor for Milwaukee School of Engineering

I started at Milwaukee School of Engineering in May of 2017. My primary responsibility is to teach, but I also help develop course curriculum, sit on the advisory board for UX and mentor students.

Recent Classes

    Psychology of Design and Digital Behaviors

    This is a course that I developed the curriculum for. Taught both in the fall and spring, this class focuses on basics of cognitive psychology, including concepts of perception, attention, memory, problem solving, expertise, reasoning, and decision-making.

    Organizational Psychology

    This class is taught by several professors each term. The majority of students at MSOE are required to take it. This course is designed to show the application of psychological aspects of managerial processes such as motivation, group processes, conflict resolution, working conditions, and organizational structure and their influences on job satisfaction.

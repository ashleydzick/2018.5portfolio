User Experience is only successful if everyone is involved and on-board. User Experience Designers aren’t the only one who has an impact on the end user — any decision made by someone at a business can be filtered through the lens of UX. As UX professionals, we have a duty to educate those around us.

I’ve been involved in building the UX community in Milwaukee since 2016; as a speaker since 2015.

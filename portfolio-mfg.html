Native iOS and Android for Milwaukee Tool

Industries: eCommerce, Manufacturing

The Challenge

The company, a large power tool manufacturer based in Milwaukee, WI., had a strong brand presence but a fractured digital team. Products, websites and applications were designed in silos and receiving a lot of negative customer feedback.

The Process

I worked with Milwaukee Tool for sixteen months, September 2015 to December 2017. Using their existing branding as a basis, I built a WCAG Level AA Compliant living style guide. I then leveraged Human Interface Guidelines and Material Design to separate the one app style into two distinct native applications. Our team then used our style guide to quickly iterate through new features.

I personally developed a new on-boarding and login process, humanized their legalese, updated their “Tool Connect” and “Add Item” workfows and several smaller other mockups.

Methods

  • Visual Design (incorporating Human Interface Guidelines and Material Design)
  • Journey Mapping / Workflows
  • Mockups
  • Prototyping
  • Style Guide

Results

Customer reviews on the Android app store went up from 2.7 to 3.6 (out of five stars). The iOS application went from a 3.9 to a 4.1.

The initial style guide I developed was adopted not just by our internal team but embraced by the developers. The dev team created a living style and code repository they could leverage in future updates.

WCAG-Compliant Software Consulting Website

Industries: Software, Technology, B2B

The Challenge

SafeNet Consulting had an out-of-date website that didn’t appropriately reflect their brand or technology capabilities. The company had two branches with completely different clientele and a half-hearted attempt to make them look similar. SafeNet’s Milwaukee office was looking to modernize their web presence so they could stop selling solely by word-of-mouth.

The Process

I analyzed both SafeNet’s current web presence, as well as their competition to come up with a list of features they had and were missing. I worked with sales and stakeholders to develop personas and basic scenarios. Using those as a lens for the website, I developed a site map and detailed information architecture.

Once the Information Architecture was developed, I brought in a designer. She worked on style and branding updates while I worked on wireframes and content. The designer then iterated on the wireframes, producing detailed prototypes that I was able to usability test. I also conducted thorough accessibility analysis, on designs and on developer implementation, as SafeNet needed a WCAG Level AA Compliant website as they competed in the healthcare and financial industries.

Methods

  • Competitive Analysis
  • Scenario and Personas
  • Information Architecture
  • Wireframes
  • Style Guide
  • Usability Testing
  • Accessibility Analysis

Results

SafeNet brought their digital presence from outdated to modern. Their SEO rankings soared and customer visits increased by 800% within the first few months after launch. We were able to produce a modern looking website that was completely WCAG Level AA compliant with many parts Level AAA compliant.
